Welcome

A Confluence of River, Land & Quiet

U Rivergate Resort & Spa is nestled amidst 60 acres of lush green landscape along the banks of River Pej, a pristine, free-flowing river. The meeting of flowing water, open landscape, scenic boulevards and serene surroundings creates a magical mood for a quick getaway — to relax, or to celebrate — whether you're travelling alone, with family, or in a group.

All rooms and villas are designed in a contemporary style, each offering a distinct view of nature — the river, the pool, or the parkland beyond.
